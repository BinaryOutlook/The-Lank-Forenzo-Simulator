# Civic Glass Theme Reference

Civic Glass is the bright, institutional theme for players who want the simulator to read like a polished public-record dashboard. It uses cool neutral surfaces, tempered teal controls, and clean translucent panels.

## Direction

- Surfaces: near-white civic glass with pale green-neutral depth, never cream or beige.
- Primary accent: teal, calibrated for buttons, selected states, and focus energy.
- Borders: visible enough to preserve panel structure on a bright canvas.
- Status colors: green success, red danger, and amber warning remain distinct from the teal accent.
- Mood: audited, official, and legible rather than cozy or decorative.

## Guardrails

- Avoid IBM-blue overlap with Armonk Blue.
- Avoid beige, parchment, or warm stationery cues.
- Keep the theme bright without washing out muted text or decision preview states.
