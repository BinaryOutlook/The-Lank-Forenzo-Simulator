The Lank Forenzo Simulator / V0.3.1

Decision-library patch with one new pack and no major rewrite

V0.3.1 is a data expansion pass. It raises the library to 100 decisions across 10 decision packs, adds regulatory_theater as a new authored lane, and keeps the existing simulation and UI architecture intact.

Patch Character

This packet is intentionally additive. The run screen, tray flow, and simulation loop remain the same. The work is mostly authored content plus the minimal registry, validation, and test updates needed to treat the new pack as first-class content.

New Pack

regulatory_theater covers oversight-management tactics: revolving-door hires, independent-review theater, hearing prep, inspector pressure, consent-order delay, and reform-office optics.

Why It Exists

The library was broad after V0.3, but still lacked a strong first-class lane for managing regulators, civic optics, and procedural respectability as a defensive strategy.

What Stayed Stable

Decision loading, validation, tray curation, and round resolution all remain structurally the same. This patch expands the authored surface instead of replacing the machinery underneath it.
