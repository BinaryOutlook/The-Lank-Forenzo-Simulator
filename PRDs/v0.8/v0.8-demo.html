V0.8 design reference

Phase the quarter. Do not crush the quarter.

This reference captures the revised round-flow direction: keep the game severe and app-like, but stop requiring every board read, decision card, consequence, and resolve control to fight inside one permanent frame.

Durable round flow

Read / Board Packet → Choose Plays → Resolve / End Round

Routes, internal view state, or a phase component are all acceptable if the player always understands where they are and how to return before final confirmation.

Read / Board Packet

Purpose: understand the quarter before choosing plays.

  • Board packet and pressure read
  • Company and personal ledgers
  • Reserve balances and recent fallout

Choose Plays

Purpose: compare all available decisions deliberately.

  • Readable costs and consequence chips
  • Clear selected and unselected states
  • Persistent selected-count feedback

Resolve / End Round

Purpose: confirm complete selections or recover cleanly.

  • Short selected-play summary
  • Missing-selection recovery path
  • Resolve only after explicit confirmation
